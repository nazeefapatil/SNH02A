MediShield's Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect:

♦ Personal Information:
• Patient demographics (name, contact details, address, etc.).
• Medical history and health records.
• Insurance information.
• Any other information required for hospital administration.
♦ Non-Personal Information:
• System usage data.
• Log files.
• Cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Collect Information:

• Information is collected directly from patients during registration and throughout their interactions with the website.
• Automated data collection tools may be used for website analytics.

3. How We Use Information:

• Provide healthcare services and manage patient records.
• Improve and optimize the functionality of the website.
• Facilitate communication between healthcare providers and patients.
• Billing and insurance processing.

4. Data Security:

• Implement industry-standard security measures to protect patient data.
• Regularly update and audit security protocols.
• Restrict access to sensitive information to authorized personnel only.

5. Sharing of Information:

• Patient information is shared within the healthcare facility for treatment purposes.
• Information may be shared with third-party service providers for website maintenance and improvement.
• Patient data is not sold or shared for marketing purposes without explicit consent or forensic research.

6. Legal Compliance:

• Comply with all relevant healthcare laws and regulations.
• Cooperate with law enforcement agencies when required by law.

7. Patient Rights:

• Patients have the right to access, modify, or delete their personal information.
• Requests for information access or modification can be made through the designated channels.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

• The Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in laws, regulations, or system functionality.
• Users will be notified of any significant changes.

9. Contact Information

If you have any comments, concerns or questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@medishield.in
