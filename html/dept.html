Departments Description

Neurology
Neurology department is a specialized medical unit dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and management of disorders affecting the nervous system.

Urology
Urology is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to the urinary tract and male reproductive system.

Ophthalmology
Ophthalmology is a medical specialty focused on the health of the eyes and visual system.

Cardiology
Cardiology is the medical specialty dedicated to the study, diagnosis, and treatment of disorders of the heart and circulatory system.

Radiology
Radiology is a medical specialty that utilizes various imaging techniques to diagnose and treat diseases and injuries within the human body.

Orthopedics
Orthopedics is a medical specialty focused on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of conditions related to the musculoskeletal system, which includes bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, tendons, and nerves.

Surgery
Surgery is a medical specialty that involves the use of operative techniques to investigate, treat, and manage various conditions, diseases, and injuries within the human body.

Pediatrics
Pediatrics is a branch of medicine that specializes in the care of infants, children, and adolescents.

Obstetrics and Gynecology
Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB/GYN) is a medical specialty that focuses on the health of the female reproductive system, encompassing both obstetrics (the care of pregnant women and their unborn babies) and gynecology (the care of the female reproductive system outside of pregnancy).

Pathology
Pathology is a medical specialty that focuses on the study of diseases and their causes, effects, and manifestations.

Anesthesiology
Anesthesiology is a medical specialty that focuses on the administration of anesthesia, which is a controlled state of temporary loss of sensation or awareness.

Nephrology
Nephrology is a medical specialty that focuses on the study and management of diseases and conditions related to the kidneys.

Psychiatry
Psychiatry is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental health disorders.

Hematology
Hematology is a medical specialty that focuses on the study, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases related to blood and blood-forming tissues.

Oncology
Oncology is a medical specialty focused on the study, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cancer.

Endocrinology
Endocrinology is a medical specialty that focuses on the study, diagnosis, and treatment of disorders related to the endocrine system.
